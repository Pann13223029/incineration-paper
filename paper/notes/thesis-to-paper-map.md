# Thesis-To-Paper Conversion Map

## Thesis Strengths To Keep

- strong empirical spine
- transparent sample construction
- calibrated claims
- two-part architecture
- clear limitations

## Thesis Traits To Compress

- long background setup
- broad literature review structure
- estimator-defense density in the main text
- repeated caveat loops
- extended policy hierarchy discussion

## Conversion Logic

### Thesis question

Who transitions into generation, and conditional on generation, what predicts efficiency?

### Paper version

How does Japan's waste-incineration transition split between selective entry into generation and bounded performance among generators?

## What Moves Out Of Main Text

- detailed robustness prose
- most workflow/reproducibility explanation
- long thesis-format signposting
- broad future-research expansion

## What Moves Into Supplement

- extra robustness detail
- estimator comparison detail
- pathway-audit rule detail
- additional sample-construction explanation

## Practical Rewrite Order

1. abstract
2. first two pages of introduction
3. methods compression
4. results compression
5. discussion rewrite
6. supplement plan
