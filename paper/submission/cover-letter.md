# Cover Letter Draft

Dear Editors,

Please consider the enclosed manuscript, *Selective Modernization and Bounded
Responsiveness in Japan's Waste-Incineration Fleet: A Facility-Level Panel
Study*, for publication as a full length article in *Waste Management*.

The paper studies Japan's municipal waste-incineration fleet using a
facility-level panel for FY2005-FY2024. Its main contribution is empirical
design rather than broad policy spectacle: it separates observed transition into
power generation from conditional performance within the generating segment. The
results show that observed entry into generation is selective rather than
diffuse, while performance within the generator segment is strongly structured
by age, scale, and utilization, with limited within-facility movement relative
to cross-facility heterogeneity.

I believe the manuscript fits *Waste Management* because it offers a clear
facility-level empirical contribution on waste-to-energy transition, keeps the
main text disciplined, and frames its broader lesson as methodological rather
than universal. The manuscript is not under consideration elsewhere.

Sincerely,  
Pann Phetra
