# Cover Letter Draft

Dear Editors,

Please consider the enclosed manuscript, *Selective Modernization and Bounded
Responsiveness in Japan's Waste-Incineration Fleet: A Facility-Level Panel
Study*, for publication as a full length article in *Waste Management*.

The paper studies Japan's municipal waste-incineration fleet using a
facility-level panel for FY2005-FY2024. Its main contribution is empirical
design: it separates observed transition into power generation from conditional
performance within the generating segment. The results show that observed entry
into generation is selective rather than diffuse, while performance within the
generator segment is strongly structured by age, scale, and utilization, with
limited within-facility movement relative to cross-facility heterogeneity.

I believe the manuscript fits *Waste Management* because it offers a clear
facility-level empirical contribution on waste-to-energy transition, keeps the
main text focused on a municipal waste-management problem, and frames its
broader lesson as methodological rather than universal. The manuscript is
original, has not been published previously, and is not under consideration
elsewhere.

Sincerely,  
Pann Phetra
